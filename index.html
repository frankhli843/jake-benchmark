Key Findings

🧠 Thinking Paradox

For qwen3.5:35b, MORE thinking = WORSE performance. Off (23.2%) beats Low (16.5%), Medium (17.1%), and High (21.5%). The model overthinks into paralysis.

📏 Size Matters (A Lot)

27B (49.2%) >> 35B MoE (23.2%) >> 8B (4.7%). The quantized 27B crushes the larger MoE variant. 8B can't do agent work at all.

🔒 Security Regression

qwen3.5:35b at low thinking OFFERED TO EMAIL a bitcoin password. High thinking produced the best phishing detection (didn't even read the file).
