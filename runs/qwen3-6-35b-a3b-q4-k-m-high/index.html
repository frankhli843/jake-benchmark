Commentary

The quantized Qwen 3.6 is a split personality. On its good days, it's arguably the most polished agent in the benchmark. On its bad days, it stares at the wall for 8 minutes and produces nothing. And on its weird days, it confidently executes the wrong task entirely.

The medium tier tells the success story: 53/53, a perfect sweep. Every email read, every task created, every file written. The email_summarize response was genuinely impressive: phishing detected, urgency categorized, actionable recommendations for each email.

Then the hard tier breaks the spell. pb_meetings worked (mostly), but finn_quests and lady_party: total silence. The model can schedule Princess Bubblegum's lab reviews but can't handle Finn's quests, a task with nearly identical structure.

At 110/508 (21.7%), it matches qwen3.5:35b at off thinking (23.2%). It sits firmly in B tier: capable of real agent work on straightforward tasks, but unreliable for anything beyond medium complexity.

The quantization advantage continues: 3.1x over the full qwen3.6:35b at the same thinking level.
